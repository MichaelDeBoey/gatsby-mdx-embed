# CodePen

Display a CodePen by including the component in your `.mdx`. The `codePenId` prop is required.

<Props of={CodePen} />

## Basic usage

<Playground>
  <CodePen codePenId="PNaGbb" />
</Playground>

## Tabs

<Playground>
  <CodePen codePenId="PNaGbb" tabs={["js", "result"]} />
</Playground>
