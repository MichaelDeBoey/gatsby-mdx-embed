# Vimeo

Display a Vimeo video by including the component in your `.mdx`. The `vimeoId` prop is required.

<Props name="Vimeo" of={Vimeo} />

## Basic usage

<Playground>
  <Vimeo vimeoId="38445453" />
</Playground>

## Skip to

<Playground>
  <Vimeo vimeoId="38445453" skipTo={{ h: 0, m: 1, s: 15 }} />
</Playground>
